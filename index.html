From there, it has been a whirlwind of changes in my contribution trajectory: want to hear about it?

About Francesca

Francesca is the WordPress Core Team Lead at Yoast, where we believe SEO is for everyone, and giving back to OSS is part of our mission. She co-led the release of WordPress.org 5.3 and 5.4. She is serving as the Core Team global representative and mentoring other women to get involved in making WordPress. One of the things she is most proud of is her blog C+B, which helped many creative female entrepreneurs find their path throughout the years. As an accomplished educator, community leader, and public speaker, you can find Francesca around the world talking about hosting, WordPress, community, open-source, women in tech, and small businesses. Now from her living room, in Torino, Italy.
